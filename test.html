Proposed Research Title
🛒Online Ordering and Customer Management System👥
A Web-Based Solution for Small and Medium-Sized Businesses  ·  Web Application
✦   ✦   ✦
💡
What is the System?

The Online Ordering and Customer Management System is a web-based platform designed to address the inefficiencies of manual order handling in small and medium-sized businesses. Traditional methods often lead to errors, delayed transactions, lost records, and poor customer service.

This system allows customers to conveniently place and track orders online while enabling business owners to manage customer data, orders, and transactions efficiently — all in one integrated platform. By automating these processes, the system reduces workload, minimizes errors, and improves operational efficiency, supporting digital transformation for local businesses.

🎯
Objectives
  • Design an online platform that allows customers to easily place and track their orders.
  • Develop a customer management module for storing and organizing customer information securely.
  • Reduce manual errors in order processing and record-keeping through automation.
  • Improve transaction speed and service quality for both customers and business owners.
  • Provide business owners with organized sales reports for better decision-making.
🏪
Client / Recipient
  • Local small and medium-sized business owners
  • Retail or Food Service Businesses
  • Contact Person: Darilyn Cariaga — Owner

System Functions
  • Customer registration and profile management for personalized service.
  • Product catalog listing with categories, descriptions, and pricing.
  • Online ordering with real-time cart and checkout functionality.
  • Order tracking and status updates for customers and owners.
  • Customer record management for organized and secure data storage.
  • Sales reporting and analytics dashboard for business insights.
  • Admin panel for managing products, orders, and customer information.
